Frequently Asked Questions

How does the mood-based matching work?

Our algorithm analyzes your preferences, gaming history, and responses to our quiz to match you with games that align with your current mood and playstyle preferences.

Can I return a digital game purchase?

Due to the nature of digital products, we generally don't offer refunds unless the game is faulty. However, if you have issues with a game, contact our support team and we'll help resolve the problem.

Do you offer technical support for games?

While we don't provide direct technical support for individual games, we can help troubleshoot common issues and direct you to the appropriate resources from game developers.

How often is your game database updated?

We update our database weekly with new releases and regularly refresh our recommendations based on community feedback and player experiences.
