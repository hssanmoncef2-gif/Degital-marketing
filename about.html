About PlayFeels

Discover the story behind our mission to revolutionize game discovery

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by Moncef Hssan, a passionate Computer Science student and gamer, PlayFeels was born from a simple frustration: the overwhelming paradox of choice in today's gaming landscape. With thousands of new games released each year, finding the perfect game that matches your mood and preferences became increasingly difficult.

Moncef noticed that traditional game stores focused on genres, graphics, and popularity, but ignored the most important factor: how a game makes you feel. That's when he decided to create a revolutionary approach to game discovery as part of his digital marketing project.

Our Mission

At PlayFeels, we believe that every gamer deserves to find their perfect match. Our mission is to eliminate the endless scrolling and guesswork from game discovery by focusing on emotional connection and personal preferences.

We combine advanced algorithms with human curation to understand not just what games you might like, but how they'll make you feel. Whether you're looking for an adrenaline rush, a relaxing escape, or an emotional journey, we'll find the perfect game for your current mood.
